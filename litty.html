Litty For Loot

Our group was given the task to solve a problem affecting a Global Challenge or finding a Global Opportunity (GCGO). We chose to address the decaying infrastructure within Johannesburg as a result of littering. Our solution was to create an app to reward users to cleaning up the city called; Litty for Loot.


Project Objective

To solve a Global Challenge, as a group we chose to address the declining infrastructure within Johannesburg.

  • ✓  Conduct background research into the issue
  • ✓  Gather data on the issue, analyse it
  • ✓  Provide a plausible solution to the issue
  • ✓  Create a wireframe protoype
  • ✓  Present the findings and wireframe
