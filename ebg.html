EBG Group

EBG Group is a company based in Cape Town that provides engineering services, refurbishment, repair, supply of motors, transformers, generators and cables. As well as the supply of new products, electrical components and alternative solutions to their clients.

The client wanted a website to increase digital footprint.The company did not have any branding and requested a simple logo to compliment the website.


The Brief

  • ❖ Create a logo
  • ❖ Website needs to inform the user about the company
  • ❖ Provide contact information
  • ❖ Modern design, but easy to navigate

Plan

The website needs to reflect the company's values are:

  • ❖  Integrity
  • ❖  Professionalism
  • ❖  Teamwork
  • ❖  Excellence

Incorporating these values into the styling by: keep it professional, simplistic and conveying that the company is approachable for collaborations.

Design

Logo

The logo is clean and simple; the company name and the use of cogs (representing engineering and industry) in the chosen colours.

Colours

Shades of blue represent: responsibility,stability, reliability, intelligence

Green represents: calming (can create trust) and growth

Font Pairing

Pairings are modern and simplistic. Using three popular fonts ensures reliability when loading different browsers and are easy to read.
