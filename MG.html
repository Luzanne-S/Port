Modern Art Gallery

Modern Art Gallery is a landing page for a fictional art gallery. The landing page has with information on the location of the gallery and contact details. The aim of creating the site was to try and practice immitating layouts and styling by only using visual information without any assets or wireframes.

I decided to add a navigation bar and logo. The images are from pexels and unsplash with the addition of the javascript plug in Animate on Scroll to add some dimension for the user while scrolling through the site.
