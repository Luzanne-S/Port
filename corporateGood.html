Corporate Givers

This project was created for a hackathon; Hack for Humanity from the Universtity of Arizona, that I participated in. My role was to be a researcher, ux and ui designer. Hack for humanity posed the question as to how to encourage civic engagment:

"Research and lived experience show that voting, belonging to an organization, and volunteering can positively impact an individual’s physical and mental health for the individual. Yet participation in these activities fluctuates and differs widely among societies across the globe."


Mind Map

The mind map created

Challenge Statement

Productive Civic Engagement within the Corporate World: Currently, corporations and working individuals lack free time and flexible schedules to participate in volunteer work.

We bridge the gap between companies/employees and volunteer/charitable activities through our app, which connects them to local/global (paid-time off) volunteering events/activities that both match their schedules and provide them the fulfillment of conributing/connecting with their community.

User Audience

Who

Our app would target partnerships with corporations (small business specifically) to build incentives for employees that volunteer (such as paid time off), encouraging a pool of new individuals who don’t often volunteer to join volunteering/charitable activities.

Why

Corporations

  • ❖  Encourage participation in volunteer/charitable work → increases company productivity, satisfaction, and moral (strengthening company brand)
  • ❖  Faster more efficient volunteer/Charity planning for corporations

Charities and Non Profits

  • ❖  Addressing resource limitations, improving project delivery, get the help they need from volunteers

Employees

  • ❖  Connecting with their community, building mental/physical health/happiness, sense of fulfillment + social networking

The solution should encompass at least three of the Humanity principles:

  • ❖ Compassion
  • ❖ Empathy
  • ❖ Respect
  • ❖ Integrity
  • ❖ Forgiveness
  • ❖ Self-reflection
  • ❖ Kindness

Customer Segment

We are targeting small to large corporations (customers), as well as their employees (user). We will also partner with nonprofits and organizers for charitable events to compile our volunteer events or activities database for customers to use during their searches.

Reaching the Audience

To reach out audience, we plan to reach out to local charities and event organizers to gather information about upcoming events and activities. Then, we will reach out to small businesses, as they are less likely to be invovled in civic activity and engagement due to lack of resources and funding that large corporations currently have.

Summary of the Solution

We bridge the gap between companies/employees and volunteer/charitable activities through our app, which efficiently connects them to local/global (paid-time off) volunteering events/activities that provide flexible scheduling and personal fulfillment of contributing to local communities.

Outline Solutions

  • Event Booking: Employees are able to choose from a list of charitable events and book themselves into the event

  • Event Chat Forum: Once an employee books an event, they are then able to chat to other event attendees in a chat room

  • Company Profiles: Each company and charitable organization has a profile that we’d utilize to match recommended opportunities to corporations, and vice versa for volunteering organizations → efficiency in opportunity searches

Uniqueness of Solution

Unique Value

We target small business corporations who don’t have the resources big companies do to host or sponsor (paid-leave) volunteer events. our target population is specific to companies who wish to contribute to civic engagement but don’t have the resources or flexible schedules to provide their employees these opportunities.

Unfair Advantage

Plan for innovation
We provide personalized/customized experience for corporations, making it eaiser for them to plan their civic engagement goals and build spread their company culture to their community: Upon subscription, companies will fill out preferences (time, goals, and values), and we will use these preferences to build a customized package: guaranteed matched opportunities every month/week, provide ads + press attention for each event, communication chat forums to connect event organizers to companies directly, and more. Our idea allows for customization, something other competitors don’t have.

Cost Structure

Spend money on advertising our platform, spent time reaching out and forming connections with small business and local non-profits/event organizers

Revenue Streams

  • ❖ Costs for platform service: companies pay to use our our event planning and chat room features. Companies may also pay for additional ads and press attention for the activities they join
  • ❖ Costs for subscriptions: pay for customized volunteer-matching and planning package (all in one package)
  • ❖ Charities can pay to find corporations who match their resource needs
