AssistMe

AssitMe is a concept for an app that allows the user to pay an individual to complete a particular task. There are a list of tasks that can be completed and the whole idea is to allow a more work life balance for people.

Background

Working people can have most of their time dedicated to their work. Work life balance can be hard to have. Running errands or spending time can take away from having mental space. In a time when particular tasks can be automated, would people welcome the idea of having daily tasks done for them.

Research Objective

To be able to understand the areas where people need relief from areas where they need errands done. To be able to create an app that can alleviate stress of errands on people.

Create part time job opportunities for the youth.

Participants

10 participants

Ages between 25 to 50 participants chosen are based on whether they have full time jobs, children etc.

Timeline

2 weeks

Questions to Ask

  • How can people have additional time during their week?
  • Can tasks or errands be done by someone else worry free?
  • What restrictions can be put in place to make users feel safe while using the app?

Research Activities

Survey

Sending participants, surveys on via whatsapp and direct messages on social media sites.

Online Research

Work Requests; from job seekers and the large amount of jobs in assistant or receptionist field

Job postings; either full time or part time assistant jobs or virtual assistant postings

User Persona

User personas' were averaged into patterns of who would be "users"

App Design

Design Style

Simplistic style

Font Pairing

Josefin + Sans, for headings

Lato for the body

Illustrations

I used the illustrations from Icons8, I chose these icons because they add a little more interest to the minimalist style.

The colours of the illustration inspired the colour scheme of the app.

Colours

magenta represents emotional balance and harmony.
true navy reflects confidence, importance and stability.

I wanted the colours to have a positive effect on the user, without being too serious but still reflect confidence.


Website Design

Landing Page Design Style

Uses the same font pairings as the app

Images & Colours

The images used replace the illustrations of the app, it is to make the user feel more engaged, making the user feel as though the app is more relatable.

The colours are more playful with orange, turquoise and lavendar
