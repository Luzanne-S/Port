New Generation Bank

Project Background

The project was from a virtual design assignment website. The main aim of the project is to understand their clients.
New Generation Bank (N.G.B) wants to research the current everyday personal banking patterns and trends of the growing millennial demographic, these are individuals born between 1981 and 1996. The bank wants to have the ability to cater to the needs of the current customers and to attract more customers to the bank.

The bank seeks to keep their customers by understanding their banking habits and also prepare for any changes that may occur in the future to this demographic for example: global changes, life changes.

Research Objective

The objective is to produce substantial evidence within a two week period that will provide enough information to make decisions on banking solutions for the specific target market. The research provided will allow the bank to gain a sense of direction into where their product and service efforts need to be placed.

Desired Output

  • ❖  Knowing the customer’s need
  • ❖  Based on customer’s needs, the bank will be able to create products/banking solutions to fulfill specific customer needs
  • ❖  Learning about trends that can create banking products to keep up with trends

Detailed Research Activities

Literature & Database Research

Timeline: 3-4 days

Roles & Responsibilities: Literature on current banking behaviors online and reviewing statistics on current customer behavior

Key Questions to ask:

  • ❖  How do our current customers do their banking? devices or in person?
  • ❖  How do customers bank; spend/save habits and on what?
  • ❖  How many customers does the bank have?
  • ❖  What are the current global and local banking trends?
  • ❖  What do (millennials) want from their bank, from public online research, social media, review sites etc.

Quantitative & Qualitative Research

Timeline: 7 days

Roles & Responsibilities: conduct online surveys ,social media polls and interviews.

Key Questions to ask:

  • ❖  What are your motivations for banking? save money, safer than cash?
  • ❖  How do you prefer to bank? desktop, in person, on an app?
  • ❖  What draws you to a bank? popularity of the bank, low fees, services offered?
  • ❖  What services would you like in the ideal bank?

Review Findings

Timeline: 3 days

Roles & Responsibilities: review data from research methodologies

Key Questions to ask:

  • ❖ What are the patterns of the current customer’s attitudes and behaviors?
  • ❖ Where can N.G.B, improve based on findings?
  • ❖ What are banking trends that customers want and need.
  • ❖ What trends could fit within the bank’s mission and values but still be innovative ?

App Mind Map

Once research was gathered and analysed and a user persona was created. I created a mind map that would summarise the findings and identifying possible spaces for product creation within the app.

App Design

The colours and style are abstract and colourful but not distracting the user or complicating the use of the app.The app journey below will communicate the way in which a user will open up the app to the user purchasing an investment product.

  • When the user opens up the app they will be open the landing page
  • They will then be sent to a Login page with the option to input their email address and password or use biometric feature to login.
  • Once logged in they will then be lead to the Homepage
  • The user can then open up their Profile that has the user's personal details, which they are able to edit.
  • The user can then open up the Accounts page.
  • The user can toggle between different bank accounts.
  • When clicked the user will then be given different call to action options and a list of recent transactions on the opened account.
  • The user can open up Investments page that provides the user with products to extend their investment portfolios.
  • Once the user clicked on Personal Savings, they are then provided products to choose from.
  • Once the user clicks on the Short Term Savings product they are provided with various savings options.
  • The user can click on the learn more button that will lead to a faq page.
  • Once the user has information about the product, they are then able to input an amount and from which bank account they would like to purchase it from.
  • When the purchase is successful,the user will then be led to a Confirmation page.
  • The user will then be lead back to the Investment page and the new product purchased will reflect on the page.

Reflection

The banking app I created was to show how the user can have a simplified and easy banking experience. It could be the bench mark for how N.G.B could create, maintain and present future products and information to the user.

There are a few shortfalls or limitations when looking at the banking sector; personal information authorization, money laundary laws,credit scores and much more. The app is to make the user's experience less formal (less jargon) and easy to use for simple banking actions.

Working on this project was interesting for me as I have only considered local banking needs and I now had the opprtunity to explore what the needs of others needs are outside of South Africa. In the future I would like to build upon the app to include bitcoin and shares information and purchasing.
