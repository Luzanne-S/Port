Litty for Loot

Project Background

Our group was given the task to solve a problem affecting a Global Challenge or finding a Global Opportunity (GCGO). We chose to address the decaying infrastructure within Johannesburg as a result of littering.
Our solution was to create an app to reward users to cleaning up the city called; Litty for Loot.
