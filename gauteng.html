Church of the Nazarene Gauteng District

The Church of the Nazarene Gauteng Disrict, holds their annual conference to present findings from the data collected from 80 churches.

Project Objective

Analyse and visualise membership and ministry data collected from 80 churches within the district so that it could be incorporated in the presentation for the upcoming conference.


The visualisations would be incorporated in a final report with other data from different departments.

Summary of Analysis

The district experienced significant growth in both membership and attendance over the past year. Membership numbers showed a steady increase of 5.23%, while weekly attendance surged by an impressive 32.23%. This upward trend was also evident across ministries, with an overall increase of 18.3%.

To gain deeper insights into this growth, detailed dashboards were developed for the Southern Zone. These dashboards serve as a model for analyzing data across all zones, enabling targeted support and informed decision-making throughout the district.

Furthermore, a 10% membership increase scenario was modeled over the next four years. This exercise serves as a valuable pre-planning tool for the district, helping to identify potential opportunities and optimize strategies for future growth.

The analysis not only confirms the district's positive trajectory, but also aligns with and strengthens the financial department's reports. Compelling visualisations were created and incorporated into a comprehensive report, which was recently presented at the annual conference.
